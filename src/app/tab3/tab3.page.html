Descriptions of Parameters
Survey
 "N/A" indicates that the surveying conditions are not met and thus the unit is not ready to be surveyed in. Once the conditions are met (for AR and ___) this field will show a timer that is used during the surveying process.
Roll
 The measured roll angle in degrees.
Pitch
 The measured pitch angle in degrees.
Yaw
 The measured yaw (or heading) angle in degrees. Note, this value may be highly inaccurate if the survey conditions are not met.
AR
 Provides an indication of the strength of the compassing fix. The minimum acceptable value is specified in parentheses next to the parameter.
D2B
 An additional parameter used to indicate the quality of the compassing fix. The maximum acceptable value is specified in parentheses next to the parameter.
CNO
 The carrier-to-noise ratio in dB-Hz. Provides an indication of the GPS signal strength.
numSvs
 The number of satellites seen by the GPS receiver.
Lat
 The measured latitude in degrees.
Lon
 The measured longitude in degrees.
Alt
 The measured height above ellipsoid (HAE) in meters.
